4. Payments & Subscriptions

The App offers two types of subscriptions through Google Play Billing:

  • Base App Subscription (required): PKR 99 per month.
    This subscription is required to use the core features of the App, such as insurance calculations and policy details.
  • PDF Subscription (optional): PKR 49 per month.
    This subscription unlocks the ability to generate, save, and share insurance calculation PDFs locally on your device.

Both subscriptions are auto-renewable and will be charged to your Google Play account unless canceled at least 24 hours before the renewal date. You can manage or cancel your subscription(s) through your Google Play account settings.
